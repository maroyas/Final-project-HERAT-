Welcome to Herat Province

About Herat

Herat Province, located in the western part of Afghanistan, is known for its rich history, diverse culture, and stunning architecture. It has been a vital cultural and trade center, with influences from Persian, Afghan, and Central Asian traditions. The province is home to beautiful landscapes, including mountains, deserts, and fertile plains.

Key Features

  • Capital City: Herat
  • Famous for: Historical landmarks and Persian architecture
  • Known as: The "Pearl of Khorasan"
  • Population: Over 1.7 million people
  • Language: Dari (Persian), Pashto
  • Climate: Semi-arid with hot summers and cold winters

Tourist Attractions

  1. The Great Mosque of Herat - A stunning example of Timurid architecture.
  2. Herat Citadel - An ancient fortress offering spectacular city views.
  3. Gawhar Shad Mausoleum - A famous mausoleum of the Timurid dynasty.
  4. Herat Bazaar - A historical marketplace with traditional Afghan crafts.
  5. Tomb of Khwaja Abd Allah Ansari - A sacred site for many Muslims.
